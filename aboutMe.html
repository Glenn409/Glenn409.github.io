About Me

Hi my name is Christopher Glenn (I'm on the right!). Some history about me, I was first introduced to programming in College. I went to Missouri State for computer science. My reason for pursuing computer science was because it had the word "computer" in its name and that I grew up on a computer as a kid. The skills I learned at school were:
- Python
- C#

I spent two years there, I ended up hating college but felt the opposite about programming. I decided to drop out, I had no further interest in school. So I moved back home worked at a lake bar in Lake of the Ozarks, Missouri. This is where I spent alot of my time growing up and maturing.

After a while, during the off seasons at work I started to learn how to program on my own terms learning through Codecademy. I ended up getting three certificates from Codecademy: Link to my Certificates.

- Build Front-End Web Apps from Scratch
- Build Web APIs from Scratch
- Freelance Website Developement

After sometime I recieved a promotion at work and business started to pick up, I had to put down programming for a bit. Which now finally brings us to this year. I realized I want to challenge and push myself further. I want to find a career that I can build on. I decided to get back into programming because I find it complex, fun, and I love the sense of accomplishment when you build something. So I decided to move to the Kansas City area and enrolled into Kansas University's Full Stack Developer Bootcamp where I hope I can pursue a career in programming.
